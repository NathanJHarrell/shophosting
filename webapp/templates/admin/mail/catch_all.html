Catch-All Mailbox

A catch-all mailbox receives all emails sent to non-existent addresses at your domain. For example, if someone sends an email to random@{{ domain }} and that address doesn't exist, the catch-all mailbox will receive it.

Select which mailbox should receive emails sent to unknown addresses, or disable to bounce them.

About Catch-All

Pros:
  • Never miss an email due to typos
  • Use unique addresses for different services
  • Easy to track where your email was shared
Cons:
  • May receive more spam
  • All unknown addresses go to one mailbox
  • Can fill up mailbox quota faster
